🚀 Quick Setup

  1. WiFi Setup: Connect to hotspot ESP32-Relay-Config (Password: 12345678).
  2. Configure: Open 192.168.4.1 and enter your Home WiFi details.
  3. Access: After reboot, clear browser cache or use Incognito mode to visit smart-switch-control.local.
  4. Activation: The license activates automatically on the first internet connection (10 Days Trial).

🔑 License & Maintenance

  • Expiration: Default validity is 10 Days. You will see a warning 7 days before expiry.
  • Extend Validity:
    1. Get a .key file from the admin.
    2. Go to Settings → Maintenance.
    3. Upload the file under "Extend License".
  • Firmware Update: You can update the device wirelessly (OTA) from the same Maintenance menu.
