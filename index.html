🚀 Quick Setup

  1. WiFi: Connect to Auto-Pump-Config (Pass: 12345678).
  2. Login: Open 192.168.4.1 to set your Home WiFi.
  3. Time: Set your GMT Offset in settings to enable Smart Scheduling (DND).
  4. Mode: Choose between Water Pump or Air Compressor mode.

🛡️ Safety & Features

  • Voltage Guard: High/Low cut-off with stabilization delay.
  • Compressor Mode: Pre/Post venting via Solenoid (GPIO 10).
  • Pump Rest: Automatic 10min cool-down after 1hr of work.
  • Dry-Run: 60s air-slug window + Auto-Retry (30/60m).
  • DND Mode: Silent operation during night hours.
