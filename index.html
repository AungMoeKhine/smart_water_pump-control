🚀 Quick Setup

  1. WiFi Setup: Connect to hotspot Auto-Pump-Config (Password: 12345678).
  2. Configure: Open 192.168.4.1 and enter your Home WiFi details.
  3. Access: After reboot, visit the device IP or http://smartpump.local to access the dashboard.
  4. Settings: Set your Tank Height and Voltage Thresholds in the device settings menu.

🛡️ Safety Features

  • Voltage Guard: Automatically stops pump if voltage is unsafe (>250V or <170V).< /li>
  • Dry-Run Protection: Detects lack of water flow and locks the pump to prevent motor burnout.
  • Precision Sensing: Uses Ultrasonic sensors for accurate water level measurement.
  • Live TFT: High-resolution monitoring right on the device hardware.
