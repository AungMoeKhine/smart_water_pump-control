🚀 Quick Setup

  1. WiFi Setup: Connect to hotspot Auto-Pump-Config (Password: 12345678).
  2. Configure: Open 192.168.4.1 and enter your Home WiFi details.
  3. Access: After reboot, visit the device IP or http://smartpump.local to access the dashboard.
  4. Settings: Set your Tank Height, Voltage Limits, and Auto-Retry logic in the settings menu.

🛡️ Safety & Features

  • Voltage Guard: Automatic cut-off (>250V or <170V).< /li>
  • Dry-Run System: Smart flow detection with configurable Auto-Retry or Lock modes.
  • LCD Dashboard: Live status on I2C LCD (2004 or 1602).
  • Hybrid Control: Control via Cloud, Local WiFi, or Physical Button.
